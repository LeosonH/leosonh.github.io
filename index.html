United States · Singapore · Australia · leoson@uchicago.edu

I find the term "data science" clunky, but a lot of my work and research does involve analyzing and managing data, and applying the scientific method to engineer insights from the clutter. My path to analytics was neither straight nor narrow, and I believe I still continue to walk the line between computer science, social sciences, and the humanities. I am most passionate about leveraging advances in technology and analytics to solve human problems, and bringing the fields closer together.

A methodologist and storyteller at heart, I am always up for a chat about causal inference, why the gradient keeps disappearing in your machine learning model, how researchers used text analysis to assert that Shakespeare was not a single author, and the metrics I used to rank the best cups of hot chocolate that you can find in Hyde Park.

I currently serve as a Research Analyst and Data Steward at the University of Chicago Urban Health Lab.

Current Location: Chicago, IL, USA
